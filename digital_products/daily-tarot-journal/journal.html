The Practice

Each morning, before checking your phone, pull one card with the question: "What does today need me to see?" Record it here immediately. In the evening, return and write what actually happened — and whether the card spoke to it.

After 90 days, you will begin to see which cards follow which, which cards appear repeatedly, and which cards presage what kinds of days. This is your personal tarot vocabulary developing. No two journals will ever be the same.

This journal contains 90 daily entry pages, a weekly reflection section, and a card frequency tracker at the back.
