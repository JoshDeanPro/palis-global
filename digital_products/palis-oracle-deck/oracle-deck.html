About This Deck

The Palis Oracle Deck contains 36 cards organized across four domains: the Inner World (matters of the self), the Connected World (relationships and bonds), the Unseen World (intuition and mystery), and the Becoming World (change, growth, and future).

Print on cardstock for best results. Cut along the dashed borders. Laminate if desired. Use alone or alongside a traditional tarot deck.

How to Use the Oracle

Shuffle with your question in mind. Draw one card for a daily message, three for past/present/future, or five for a comprehensive read. Trust your immediate feeling upon seeing the card — that first flash of knowing is your intuition responding before the mind can interfere.

Unlike tarot, oracle cards carry no reversed meanings. Hold each card upright and read the energy that calls to you within it.
