How to Use This Tracker

Print one page per moon cycle (roughly every 29.5 days). Track your intentions, emotional weather, energy levels, and card pulls through each of the four major phases: New Moon, First Quarter, Full Moon, and Last Quarter. Over 3-6 months, patterns will emerge that reveal your personal relationship with the lunar cycle.

You don't need to be precise about exact moon dates — working within 2-3 days of each phase is perfectly aligned with the energy.
