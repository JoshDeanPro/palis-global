The Architecture of Correspondence

Astrology and Tarot share the same root language — both are systems for mapping the invisible forces that shape human experience. The Astro-Tarot Blueprint teaches you to read them together, creating a unified picture that is far more nuanced than either system alone.

Your birth chart tells you the terrain. The Tarot tells you the weather. Together, they reveal the full landscape of your moment.

Part III · Reading Your Natal Signature

To find your personal Tarot signature cards, you need three things from your birth chart: your Sun sign, your Moon sign, and your Rising (Ascendant) sign.

Sun Card
Your Conscious Self
The card of your Sun sign represents who you are becoming — your ego's highest expression and the core challenge of this lifetime.
Moon Card
Your Emotional Body
The card of your Moon sign reveals your instinctive responses, what you need to feel safe, and your unconscious patterns in relationship.
Rising Card
Your Outer Face
The card of your Rising sign shows how others perceive you and the mask you wear in the world — sometimes before even you know you're wearing it.
Chart Ruler Card
Your Soul's Governor
Find the planet that rules your Rising sign. Its corresponding Major Arcana card is your chart ruler card — a deeply personal signature of your entire soul path.
