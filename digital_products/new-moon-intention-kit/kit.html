Working with the New Moon

The New Moon is the darkest point of the lunar cycle — and therefore the most potent for planting intentions. What you call in at the New Moon has the energy of the entire cycle to build and grow. Use each worksheet within 48 hours of the exact New Moon.

Each monthly worksheet is customized for the sign the New Moon falls in, because a Scorpio New Moon calls for very different intentions than a Gemini New Moon.
